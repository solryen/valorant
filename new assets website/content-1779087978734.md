Common questions

## What mothers _ask us most_.

If you're wondering, someone else was too.

Is Willo free?

Willo is **free to install and free to onboard**. That includes getting to know you, creating your profile, finding your baby's current developmental phase, and exploring the app's core layout.

Some of the ongoing features are part of a premium membership, because they cost real money to run beautifully. We keep pricing intentionally simple and affordable. Think **less than one pediatrician visit for an entire year** of guidance, sleep support, AI chat, and community. No ads. No data selling. Just a better experience for mothers.

What age range is Willo for?

Willo covers **birth through age six**. The 35 developmental phases are designed to accompany your baby from the first day home through toddlerhood and into early childhood. Most mothers join during pregnancy or in the first three months of baby's life, when the need for a calm, knowing companion is strongest, and many stay on through toddler years for the ongoing educational content and community.

What are the 35 developmental phases?

The 35 phases are named developmental windows your baby moves through between birth and age six. Each phase is paired with the social, physical, and cognitive changes happening at that moment, plus gentle guidance on what to expect and how to support it.

Rather than the generic "week 24 milestone checklist" many parenting apps offer, Willo gives you a **map of who your baby is becoming**. When a hard week hits, you'll know it's a phase, not a failure.

How is Willo different from Huckleberry, Napper, or Wonder Weeks?

Most parenting apps are trackers or sleep calculators. Willo is a **companion**.

Huckleberry and Napper focus on sleep log analysis and routines. Wonder Weeks focuses on 10 mental leaps in the first 20 months. Willo covers **35 developmental phases across six years**, adds a warm AI assistant (Ask Willo), a private memory book, a mood journal, sleep sounds, phase-matched games, and a supportive community. All inside one calm, ad-free iOS app. The design is warm editorial, not clinical dashboard.

How do I know if my baby is in a developmental leap or regression?

Willo watches your baby's age and phase and **tells you automatically**. The home screen shows the current phase with a gentle progress bar, notes common behaviors during that window (extra fussiness, sleep disruption, appetite changes), and offers phase-matched support. No need to guess or Google. You'll know ahead of the change, not after it.

If you're mid-regression tonight, Ask Willo can walk you through what's normal, what often helps, and what to watch for.

Is Ask Willo a real doctor or AI?

Ask Willo is an **AI parenting assistant** trained on developmental science and calibrated to the tone of a warm, knowledgeable friend. It is not a doctor and does not give medical diagnoses. For anything clinical, it will gently suggest speaking with your pediatrician.

Where it shines: the "am I a bad mom" questions, the "what's happening this week" questions, and the 2am "why won't she sleep" questions.

Can I use Willo during pregnancy?

Yes, and many mothers do. Pregnancy is when the quiet comes before the storm. You can set up your profile in advance, explore the phases you're about to experience with your baby, browse the memory book, and use Ask Willo for the early questions you'd rather not ask in a group chat.

How does the Memory Book work?

Each day you can capture one photo of your baby. Willo stamps it with the date, developmental phase, and age, and organizes your daily frames into a year-long memory book by phase.

All photos stay **on your device**. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is shared. It's yours, forever.

Is my data private?

Yes. Photos stay on your device. Mood logs and journal entries stay on your device. Community posts can be made with an anonymous handle. Ask Willo conversations are not tied to any identity beyond your account.

**We do not sell data. We do not run ads.** The business model is subscription, not surveillance.

Does Willo work if my baby is premature?

Yes. During setup you can enter your baby's adjusted age (gestational minus premature weeks), which is the age most pediatricians recommend using for developmental tracking. Your phase progression follows the adjusted age so the content meets your baby where they actually are.

Can my partner use Willo too?

Absolutely. Willo works beautifully for partners, co-parents, and family members who want to follow along. Today that happens through separate accounts, with shared profiles on the roadmap. A lot of dads and partners use Ask Willo to get up to speed without feeling judged for asking.

Does Willo donate to charity?

Yes. A portion of every dollar Willo App earns goes to **children's disability care**. Motherhood is a community effort, and the more mothers feel held, the more families we can help feel the same way.

Is Willo available on Android?

Not yet. Willo is **iOS only** today, on iPhone and iPad, available in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. Android is on the roadmap. Email help@meetwillo.app if you'd like to be told when it launches.

Who built Willo?

Willo is built by **Sonora Labs**, an independent studio that makes apps and music focused on nourishing attention and quiet wellbeing. The team's background is in music that calms and design that breathes. Willo is the result of applying that practice to the first six years of motherhood.
